Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how your information is handled.

1. Data Collection & Use

The FFMI Calculator is designed to be privacy-first. **We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII)** entered into the calculator fields (Weight, Height, Body Fat %). All calculations are performed instantly in your browser.

2. Non-Personal Data (Analytics)

We use lightweight, privacy-focused analytics (e.g., Plausible or basic Google Analytics) to track website performance (e.g., page views, button clicks) and improve the user experience. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

3. Local Storage (Optional)

If we implement features like "Save Profile" or "Compare Results" in a future phase, we may use your browser's **Local Storage** to save data locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains entirely under your control.

4. Compliance

While we collect minimal data, we strive for compliance with global privacy regulations, including the basic principles of GDPR and CCPA, by providing transparency and choice regarding non-personal data processing.
